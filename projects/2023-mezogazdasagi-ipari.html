We have plans for improving energy-efficiency in the field of agriculture as well which would only require an external need and/or a cooperating partner to kick off. Our plan is not based on digitalization, but rather on the development and modification of technology in an energy-efficient way. In addition to energy-efficiency there can also be product and production development based on the needs of the market.
