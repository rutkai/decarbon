Energy efficient technologies not only have a financial benefit due to their reduced cost but are also important factors for economic operators. In addition, a reduced environmental impact is becoming more and more of a significant demand from end users.

As such, the only possible path to the future is to protect our environment through energy efficiency.

Political decision-makers are put under increasing pressure by environmental organizations in order to achieve their goals to protect the environment. However, they do so without developing environmentally friendly technologies or providing substantiated guidance in the matter which makes the question of developing these technologies all the more important.
